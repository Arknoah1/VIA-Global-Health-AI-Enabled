# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages (`/`, `/about`, `/contact`)
- Public catalog and product pages (`/catalog`, `/products/:slug`)
- Public legal/info pages (`/privacy-policy`, `/return-policy`)
- Shared technical SEO foundations (robots, sitemap, canonical, hreflang, social tags, favicon, structured data, AI crawler readiness)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated admin pages (`/admin/**`)
- Admin APIs and internal tools
- Quote-tracking utility flow (`/track-quote`) unless it introduces shared technical SEO issues

## Target audience
- Distributors, healthcare providers, and NGOs sourcing medical equipment, especially across Africa and other low-resource settings.

## Primary keywords
- medical equipment Africa
- medical equipment supplier
- global health medical equipment
- medical products catalog
- product-specific equipment keywords
