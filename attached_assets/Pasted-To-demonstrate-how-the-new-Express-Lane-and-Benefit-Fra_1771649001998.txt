### **Why this resolved the "Engagement Cliff":**

1. **Immediate Gratification:** By providing the $3,850 price immediately after she said "ready to buy," Amara rewarded her intent rather than putting up a "Name/Email" gate.
2. **Avoided the Circular Trap:** Instead of asking for her organization type five times (like in Quote #23), Amara moved to a default setting after the second vague answer, preserving the sale.
3. **Contextual Value:** Shipping data was provided *before* the customer had to commit to further questions, keeping the "Value-First" flow intact.
